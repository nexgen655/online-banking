Quick Help

    What is Zelle®? expand_more

    Zelle® is a fast, free, and easy way to send money to and receive money from people you trust, directly from your bank account.

    Contact not yet enrolled in Zelle? expand_more

    You can still send money to them. They will receive an email or text notification with instructions on how to enroll to receive the money.

    How do I receive money using Zelle®? expand_more

    You can receive money directly into your checking or savings account. If you're enrolled, you'll receive a notification when money is sent to you.
